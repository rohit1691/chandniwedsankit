Our Story

 

Rollover the timeline

Highlight 1

July, 2015
Onelittleword says he met his wife on a dating app when she swapped her right & it all started with the witty line of “Hi, you seem like a die hard fan of Game of thrones”. “It was on 18th July 2015 & it seems like yesterday” crazy how these things go. It landed me my dream girl.

Highlight 2

November, 2015
Countless conversation began mostly on texts - phase of knowing each other. Late night stories about supernatural stuff was one of my favorite. From friends we became good friends. He was my diet counselor who taught me how to eat healthy & be healthy.

Highlight 3

March, 2016
First date- Starbucks- Metropolis Gurgaon when we first saw each other in real. He was wearing his favorite color & character T-shirt (Sheldon) just to impress her(haha), but she was different & complicated. It all went good our “first date”. After some days, with all courage he proposed her by saying “I wanna grow old with you”.

Highlight 4

April, 2017
The day when our families met, “nervous moments “. Everything went extraordinarily smooth. Though it’s tough to convince parents but it’s not impossible. It was the time to be very patient & positive & everything went well. Both families liked one other and then we started dating officially.

Highlight 5

May 2017
Our first trip came, to the land of hippies-Kasol. She was gazing through the window to the mighty ice capped peaks day & night. The sound of River Parvati was so engrossing. Most memorable was, her wearing my pullover. I fell more in love. I could not sleep whole night because reality was better than dreams.

Highlight 6

June, 2017
When we officially became partners for life. It was the day of stopping ceremony A.K.A "Roka"

July, 2017
